About

Zephaniah Galloway is a designer and creative from Cleveland and a graduate of Howard University. Her work sits at the intersection of technology, culture, and storytelling. With a background in UX design, web development, and Black Studies, she enjoys creating digital experiences and content that feel thoughtful and human.
